Throwback Game

HOW TO GAIN YOUR LUCK

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four marshmallows below. Each marshmallow is given a specific luck value. By clicking on a marshmallow you will reveal its luck value and add the luck value to your player score.

Win by matching your player score to the goal score set. Guess too much and you lose.

Every time you win or lose, the game will randomly select new marshmallow luck values and require you to meet a new goal.
